# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simplified version of the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## TL;DR

Be kind. Be patient. Disagree about the code, not the person.

## Specifics

- Treat everyone with respect, regardless of background.
- Critique ideas, not people. If someone misses a thing, point at the thing.
- Assume good faith when reading someone else's contribution.
- If a conversation gets heated, step away and come back.
- Harassment, hate speech, and personal attacks are not tolerated and will
  result in a permanent ban from the project.

## Enforcement

Issues can be raised privately to **Anthony Harwelik** at
`aharwelik@gmail.com` (subject line `Aquarium CoC`). I'll respond within
a week and act based on what makes the project healthier.

That's it. No tribunals, no decision matrices — just don't be a jerk and
this stays a friendly place for everyone who wants to look at fish.
